ABOUT ME

My name is Linnea and I am 22 years old. I am born and raised here in Jönköping. I live together with my partner Oscar and our cat Tellus. Tellus is a Bengal cat that is 5 years old, he is very cozy and loves to snuggle under a blanket with me. I started to study New Media Design because I wanted to work with something creative. At first I did not know exactly what I wanted, but then last fall I took a web development course where I realised that it was something I really wanted to work with. My understanding of web development is still very vague, but I look forward to deepen my experience.
